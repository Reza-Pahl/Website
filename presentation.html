The Problem

Identifying parts in complex machines is slow and error-prone

Large industrial machines with tens of thousands of parts and decades of documentation create real operational friction — for technicians and customers alike.

🔩

30,000+ Parts Per Machine

Many components look nearly identical. Without visual context, technicians waste time cross-referencing flat documentation to find the right part number.

📋

30+ Year Old Machines

Legacy machines have accumulated layers of outdated docs. The older the machine, the harder it is to match a physical component to accurate records.

⚠️

Manual, Static Catalogs

Parts catalogs are built by hand from eBOM exports — flat, disconnected from live ERP data, and offering no visual or spatial search capability.

Before

How Parts Are Identified Today

A multi-system, manual process that degrades with machine age and scale.

After

The New Way: 3D Interactive SPC

The same source data — enriched with CAD, assembly structure, and ERP — becomes a searchable, interactive 3D spare parts catalog.
