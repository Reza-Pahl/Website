Frontline.io Daily Digest — Buying Signals

A lightweight AI agent that tracks news and operational signals for ICP accounts, then sends a daily email digest to sales teams.

What it does

This workflow monitors public news, press releases, funding events, leadership changes, manufacturing expansions, and other “buying signals” across a list of target accounts.

Each morning, the system generates a clean, prioritized digest with short summaries, links, and recommended outreach angles — then sends it automatically to the sales team.

How it works

  • Scrapes news + signals via custom APIs, Tavily, and AI summarization.
  • Filters signals by industry, geography, and ICP relevance.
  • Classifies priority using an LLM “relevance ranker.”
  • Bundles everything into a single daily email.

Why it's useful

  • Reduces manual prospect research.
  • Improves timing and relevance of outbound messaging.
  • Gives reps a reason to reach out — every single day.
  • Keeps big deals warm with signal-based touchpoints.
